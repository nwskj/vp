About WildGuard

We are dedicated to protecting endangered species and their natural habitats worldwide.

Our Mission

WildGuard works tirelessly to protect endangered species and preserve their natural habitats through scientific research, community engagement, and policy advocacy.

Founded in 2012, we've grown into an international organization with projects in over 40 countries, focusing on the most vulnerable wildlife populations.

We believe that every species has a right to exist, and biodiversity is essential for a healthy planet and human survival.
